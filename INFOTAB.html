INFO WORK

Victor Masotti (2006), also known as BrickBeads, is a self-taught Italian artist and sculptor based in London. He started exploring 3D softwares such as SketchUp Pro and architecture at the age of 12, generative art at 15 and sculpture at 16.

Masotti's practice focuses on the collision between generative/computer-generated art, geometry, colors and the human error, producing unexpected results as a consequence of his systematic and impulsive approach to art.
