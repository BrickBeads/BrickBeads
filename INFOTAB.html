VICTOR MASOTTI
INFO WORK

Victor Masotti, (born September 20, 2006, Rimini, Italy), is an Italian artist and sculptor based in London since 2015, best known for his work with geometry, colors and shapes to create colorful objects, sculptures, paintings and drawings. Fascinated by the concrete art and neo-concretism movements, Masotti's art focuses on bringing color and art to every inch of the world.
